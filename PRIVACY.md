# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 30, 2026

## Introduction

UMarkdownPreview ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our macOS application.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

UMarkdownPreview operates entirely on your local device. We do not:

- Collect, store, or transmit any personal data
- Track your usage or behavior
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Display advertisements
- Require user accounts or registration
- Access your files without your explicit permission

## Local Data Processing

All file processing occurs locally on your device:

- Markdown files are opened and previewed directly on your Mac
- No file content is uploaded to external servers
- QuickLook and Thumbnail extensions process files locally
- All rendering is performed on-device

## Permissions

The app requests minimal permissions necessary for functionality:

- **File Access**: To open Markdown files you explicitly select
- **QuickLook Extension**: To provide preview functionality in Finder
- **Thumbnail Extension**: To generate file icons in Finder

These permissions are used solely for the described features and do not enable data collection.

## Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services, analytics providers, or advertising networks.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. Your Markdown files remain entirely under your control on your local device.
